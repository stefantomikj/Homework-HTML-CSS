Requirements for co-borrower: to be a member of the immediate family other person who is a client receiving salary/pension on a SB account; to be a client with an existing loan product in SB or at any other bank and with good credit history Requirements for Individuals employed abroad, citizens of the RNM Loan borrowers or co-borrowers may be accepted as such provided that they are members of the immediate family and are employed in the Republic of North Macedonia. They should also submit an Employment Contract and the latest annual tax statement submitted to the tax authority in the country where they are employed (the documents have to be translated by an authorized translator).
